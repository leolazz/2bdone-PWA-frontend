2BeDone

Is a progressive web app for tracking projects & tasks

It was developed as a learning exercise for Ionic & Angular

Next

Home Tab

Allows you view upcoming tasks and projects from a month or week view

Selecting one of the items will provide quick details & allow you to navigate directly to it

Whichever tab you are on you click the + icon and create a new item

Next

Projects & Tasks Tabs

Lists all their respective up coming items.

Each has a side bar where you can filter through completed and un completed items, or search for a particular item.

Clicking on one allows you edit it's details.
